DATA COLLECTION APPROACH:
This study collects ANONYMOUS data only. No personally identifiable 
information is collected or stored at any point.

DATA COLLECTED:
- Anonymous gameplay interactions and decisions
- Anonymous conversations with AI agents (names/identifiers written by participants in a conversation will be anonymized)
- Anonymous questionnaire responses
- Session metadata (duration, timestamps - not linked to participant's session ID)

ANONYMIZATION PROCESS:
- Participants receive random session IDs
- No connection between session IDs and participant identity
- No IP addresses, device IDs, or browser fingerprints collected
- Questionnaire responses collected without identifying information

DATA STORAGE & SECURITY:
- All data stored locally
- No cloud storage or external transmission
- Access restricted to researcher and thesis supervisor

DATA RETENTION:
- Anonymous data retained for thesis duration
- Deletion after thesis defense

PARTICIPANT RIGHTS:
Since data is collected anonymously, individual data cannot be:
- Accessed by specific participant
- Modified for specific participant  
- Deleted for specific participant
(We cannot identify which data belongs to which participant)

VOLUNTARY PARTICIPATION:
- Participation is entirely voluntary
- You may stop participating at any time
- Once submitted, anonymous data cannot be withdrawn (we cannot identify it)
